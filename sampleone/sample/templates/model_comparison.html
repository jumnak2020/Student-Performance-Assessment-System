Building institution-specific models ensures higher accuracy and follows data science best practices.

📚 Understanding the Metrics

R² Score: Measures how well the model explains the data. Range 0–1. Higher is better.
MSE (Mean Squared Error): Average squared difference between actual and predicted values. Lower is better.
RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error): Square root of MSE. Same units as target variable. Lower is better.
CV Mean (Cross-Validation Mean): Average R² across 5 train/test splits. Shows generalisation ability.
